# Problem Statement Selected

## 🎯 Community Hero (Hyperlocal Problem Solver)

### Overview
In urban and semi-urban communities, identifying and resolving local infrastructure and utility problems (e.g., potholes, water pipe leakages, broken streetlights, illegal garbage dumping) is often a slow and inefficient process. Citizens lack a transparent, real-time method to report issues, and municipal corporations are overwhelmed by manual triage, leading to delayed action and citizen dissatisfaction.

### Project Goal
**CivicFlow** is built to solve this hyperlocal problem by enabling citizens to quickly identify, report, and track community issues. At its core, CivicFlow leverages a state-of-the-art **AI multi-agent system** to automate the municipal triage workflow. It autonomously:
1. **Assesses** visual evidence of reported issues.
2. **Routes** formal tickets to the correct municipal departments with appropriate severity levels.
3. **Analyzes** local data trends to provide predictive recommendations for infrastructure maintenance.
